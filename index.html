The SkiPod holds it all!

All what? All of your essential snowsports equipment: skis/snowboard, boots, poles, gloves, helmet, goggles, even those bulky snow pants and snow jacket. The SkiPod holds it all within a single hard-sided container—no more separate bags for your boots and your skis or board—the SkiPod holds about 2 ½ bags worth of gear in one state of the art, ultra-light pod.

The SkiPod is Ultra Portable.

You're probably picturing a traditional ski bag and boot bag and thinking: this is going to be big and heavy. No—not at all! The SkiPod not only holds all of your gear, it does it so efficiently that a SkiPod is small enough to maneuver easily wherever you may go.

The SkiPod stands by you for your entire trip.

This isn't an ordinary piece of luggage. The SkiPod is a portable ski locker—a wardrobe for your gear. As much a ski valet as it is a piece of luggage, the SkiPod enhances the entire travel experience by making packing, transporting, organizing, retrieving and storing of ski equipment easier for the ski traveler.
